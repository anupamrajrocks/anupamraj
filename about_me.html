About Me


Hey everyone, I am 'Anonymous' but sometimes people call me Anupam Raj. Basically, I am an undergraduate who wants to introduce himself to this world by his creative ideas. I am from Bihar which is famous for its ancient histoey, once it was considered as a centre of power, learning, and culture. Currently, I am working on front-end development and I have to start back-end development. I have also done some work in data analysis and have a good knowledge of python libraries i.e Numpy, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn. In data analysis, I love to represent the data by plotting various types of graphs. I am also some intrested in vector illustration and 3D graphic designing.
